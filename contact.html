Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the sourcing process typically take?

The timeline varies depending on product complexity and requirements. Typically, from initial inquiry to sample approval takes 3-4 weeks. Mass production then follows based on order quantity and factory capacity.

What are your commission rates?

Commission structures are customized based on order volume, product type, and service scope. We offer competitive rates for both one-time purchases and long-term partnerships. Contact us for a detailed quote.

Do you handle all export documentation?

Yes, we manage all aspects of export logistics including customs documentation, shipping coordination, and delivery arrangements. We can arrange FOB Shenzhen or door-to-door delivery options.

What if I'm not satisfied with the first order?

We stand behind our quality standards. If you're not satisfied, we'll work with you to address issues and offer favorable terms for subsequent orders to ensure your complete satisfaction.

Can you help with long-term supplier relationships?

Absolutely. We specialize in establishing and managing long-term partnerships with verified suppliers. We handle ongoing communication, quality monitoring, and continuous improvement initiatives.

What industries do you serve?

We work across diverse industries including electronics, textiles, machinery, consumer goods, and more. Our expertise spans multiple sectors, allowing us to source products from various manufacturers.
